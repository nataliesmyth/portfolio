About Me

Hi, I’m Natalie (she/her). I'm a people person who loves programming. When it comes to programming, bartending, or any other work,you won’t find me just going through the motions; life is too short. If I’m going to spend my time doing something, I want to do it right. My empathetic nature drives my desire to leave things better than I found them, and makes it glaringly obvious that good work demands a good user experience.

After all, good content is good for nothing if people are unable or unwilling to consume it. I have high energy levels, boundless enthusiasm, and I believe in human beings capacity for innovation and creativity. I'm excited to work with a team who cares about their work and wants to make great things happen.
